Arts Library Open House Branding

For the opening house event for the Arts Library at UCLA, I volunteered to assist in making branded merch for the library for freebies during the event. Designing for potential stickers, erasers, and buttons, I created several designs for the Communications board to select for the event. 

In connection to the Open house event being the same week as Valentine's, this was the final decision made into stickers and buttons! (Still available as freebies today!)
