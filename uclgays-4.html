La Gente Newsmagazine Fall Issue '22

I contributed visuals myself such as the cover image, as well as making, editing, and overseeing the process of creating the overall magazine layout of our digital spring issue. In addition, I worked with the visual team to edit and provide feedback on their pieces to encourage growth and a healthy learning environment.
