La Gente Newsmagazine - Reflections cover

I was given the opportunity to design the cover for the Winter 2020 zine issue for La Gente Newsmagazine, where we handed them out at Bruinwalk and it was greatly received, selling out of copies before our last day of tabling. In theme of the zine title, I reflect back on the fact that later on, once I became editor, an intern shared that she joined La Gente one year later because she happened to pick up a copy of the issue and really loved the cover that she kept it and hung it on her wall. She decided that she would apply. 

Made with Procreate and Photoshop.
