about me

Haven Jovel Morales

student, writer, artist

Hello, welcome to my website! I am Haven Jovel, a Salvadoran-American student attending the University of California, Los Angeles as a fourth year. I am studying Cognitive Science with a minor in Digital Humanities. I have a deep interest in design, UI/UX, game development, social justice, and AI/machine learning. I am also interested in the societal and intrapersonal impacts the internet and digitalization of modern society has on individuals, the moral and ethical dilemma behind the development of the internet. I hope to pursue a higher degree where I can research and challenge these ideas, whilst pursuing my passion as an aspiring artist, writer, and designer. 

I also find enjoyment in coding, web development, and data analysis. I also enjoy collaboration and working on social justice projects. I have a deep passion for art and hope wherever I find myself, I am able to express my creativity and admiration for it and am driven to improve myself everyday. During my last year in college and postgrad, I hope to gain work experience within related fields of my interests. I hope to integrate my passion for creativeness, diversity, accessibility, and social justice wherever I find myself at. Most importantly, I want to give back to my community and my family, and of course, make sure I can feed the glutton of a cat I have.
